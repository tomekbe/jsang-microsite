Today, Saab operates across the world, and across an ever-wider range of products, sectors and customers.

Whenever and wherever people come into contact with Saab, we need them to know what we stand for and what binds our business operations together.

To achieve this, we have a Saab Brand Platform, to help you to present Saab clearly and consistently, to all our audiences, in all our communications, all over the world.

Everyone at Saab is responsible for portraying the Saab brand faithfully and consistently in all channels. Because everything we say or do will either strengthen (or weaken) our brand.

And a strong brand makes for a stronger and more profitable company.

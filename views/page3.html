Presenting Saab in a clear and consistent way requires flexible communications that can respond to the different needs and expectations of everyone we come in contact with – through whichever channel they see us.

So, from our Brand Essence, we’ve developed a communications framework that is relevant to all of our stakeholders – from our employees and partners to governments and other customers.

To enable communications to be tailored to our stakeholders’ varying needs, the framework consists of stakeholder contexts and narratives. A stakeholder context is a key issue that concerns our stakeholders. A stakeholder narrative is Saab’s response to that issue.

CONTEXT, NARRATIVES AND LINKS

Context:My stakeholders want to make the world a safer place.

  • Protect people and society from existing and emerging threats.
  • Provide protection through cutting edge products and solutions

Narrative: Keeping people and society safe.

  • Saab believes everybody has the right to feel safe.
  • Saab exists to provide safety and security through innovative thinking.

Context: My stakeholders have changing environments and changing needs.

  • Keeping one step ahead of today’s complex defence and security challenges.
  • They want technology that is prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

Narrative: Anticipating tomorrow.

  • Saab are continuously thinking about stakeholders’ future needs.
  • Saab's products and solutions are developed with future capabilities in mind.

Context: My stakeholders need to enhance their capabilities with smarter solutions.

  • Using smarter solutions to improve their outcomes.
  • Gaining an advantage through practical and cost-effective solutions.

Narrative: Giving your capability the edge.

  • Saab delivers enhanced and sustained capabilities by innovative thinking.
  • Saab’s pragmatic approach to problem solving can improve your performance and effectiveness.

Context:My stakeholders need partnering and collaboration.

  • Forming ties with businesses who share technology, ambition and pride.
  • Committing to developing local markets and communities.

Narrative: Bringing ideas together.

  • Saab has a firm belief in investing in local businesses and industries.
  • Saab believes in sharing technology, ideas and thinking.

Our brand essence

The thinking edge is our Brand Essence. It’s what makes us different, and able to fulfill our mission. It forms the core of all our communications.

In a rapidly changing world where new threats continue to emerge, we firmly believe in the right of countries, institutions and individuals to protect their freedom and safety. It’s a belief born out of our history, for over 75 years our products and solutions have been helping to create safer societies around the world.

We’re building on that heritage by empowering our world-class employees to break new ground. By thinking innovatively we’re pushing intellectual and technological boundaries - keeping us ahead of today’s complex defence and security challenges.

Our thinking also embodies drive, expertise and trust – values that help us work collaboratively and pragmatically together with our customers and partners. But it's the products and solutions that emerge from our thinking that give us a distinct advantage over our competitors - our thinking edge.
